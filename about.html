About CRM with Eon de Vos

Are you a professional pilot with a South African Comm or ATP operating in General Aviation?

Whether you fly helicopters under Part 127 or 128, fixed wings under Part 135 or any other related GA flying – this CRM course is for you.

I have been involved in Human Factors/CRM training for a major airline since 2003 and started offering a specially tailored CRM course for GA pilots in 2008.

I strongly believe that simply adhering to the legal requirement may very easily become an exercise in futility, where the objective simply becomes “ticking the boxes”.

I have my own SACAA-approved CRM manual, which incorporates current theory and practical examples for the initial course.

Every year I also present a brand-new CRM Refresher covering the latest trends and addressing problems specific to the GA environment.

Meet Eon


Eon is a line pilot flying Boeing 737s for a prominent airline and has been a Human Factors/CRM instructor for 17 years. Leveraging his experience in both airline and general aviation, he offers CRM courses to GA pilots, with particular focus on Helicopter and Single Pilot operations. His background in radio and TV gives him an advantage in presenting to pilots.

Eon’s flying career spans a range of operations. He has flown competitive and airshow aerobatics, winning several South African Aerobatic Championships. He has flown flight instruction, charter, and SA Air Force operations, retiring as a Major. Eon has flown extensive helicopter operations, having started SA’s first ever Electronic News Gathering (ENG) operation, and has flown in the airlines since the early 2000s.

He has over 16000 hours in his logbook and more than 60 types of aircraft and helicopters on his license. He holds SA ATP licenses for both fixed-wings and helicopters, as well as a FAA fixed-wing ATP.
