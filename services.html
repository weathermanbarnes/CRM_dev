The SACAA CATS and CARS stipulate that pilots operating aircraft in Part 121, 127 or 135 should undergo "crew resource management training including human factors, risk analysis and error management training". Operators should ensure that new pilots undergo initial CRM training followed by annual recurrency courses. I offer both approved initial and recurrent CRM training.
